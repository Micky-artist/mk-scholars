East African University Rwanda is pleased to announce that registration for the May 2025 Intake is now open. Students who wish to study in the academic year 2024-2025 are invited to apply for a variety of programs designed to equip them with the knowledge and skills needed in today’s job market. Whether you are looking for full-time, evening, or weekend classes, we have flexible learning options to suit your schedule.

Additionally, we are excited to offer scholarships to qualified students, providing an opportunity for them to achieve their educational dreams without financial barriers. We encourage all interested applicants to register as soon as possible to secure the scholarship.

SCHOLARSHIP BENEFITS

• Full or partial tuition fee coverage for eligible students.
• Access to high-quality education from experienced lecturers.
• Opportunity to study in a supportive and modern learning environment.
• Flexible study options: Day, Evening, and Weekend classes.
• Career guidance and internship placement support.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

• Must have completed high school (Advanced Level Certificate or equivalent).
• Must meet the minimum academic requirements for the chosen program.
• Must submit all required documents before the deadline.
• Must demonstrate financial need for scholarship consideration.

AVAILABLE PROGRAMS
East African University Rwanda offers a wide range of programs relevant to the job market, including:

• Film Making & Film Production
• Industrial Art & Design
• Leisure Tourism & Hotel Management
• Mass Communication
• Business Studies (Accounting, Finance, Economics, Marketing, Procurement, Credit Management, and Human Resource Management)
• Education (History & Geography, Mathematics & Computer Science, Mathematics & Physics)

Additionally, we offer a Professional CPA (Certified Public Accountant) program and various short courses approved by the Rwanda TVET Board (RTB), including:

• Animation and Motion Graphics
• Arts and Innovation Design
• Filmmaking and Film Production
• Multimedia Journalism
• Culinary Arts
• Leisure Tourism
• Logistics and Supply Chain Management
• Rooms Division

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
• Copy of National ID or Passport
• High school diploma or equivalent certificate
• Academic transcripts
• Passport-size photos (2)

APPLICATION PROCEDURES
1. Visit the university headquarters in Nyagatare, Kigali Campus in Remera Gisementi, or apply online by opening the application link at the bottom.
2. Fill in the application form with accurate details.
3. Attach the required documents.
4. Submit your application before the deadline.
5. Await confirmation and further instructions from the admissions office.

APPLICATION DEADLINE
All applications must be submitted before April 20, 2025.
